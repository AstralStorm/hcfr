HCFR Colorimeter

Mounting

 
Drill holes

Board is nearly ready. You have 98 holes to drill (12 less is you chose CMS). Note holes at 2.5mm for fixing and USB, and hole at 6.0mm for IR led..

 

 

CMS

CMS implantation schema is available in PDF format.

The most delicate part is mounting CMS, particularly TCS230 sensors. Take care about orientation.

Tips: the sensor ground pin is easy to see, it has a reversed L shape inside the SOIC box.


  

 

Traditional components

Traditional implantation schema is available in PDF format.

Begin with the four straps. Note the one which is partially under 28 pin support.

Then, the five resistors, then the 28 pin support, push-buttons, condensers (if not in CMS), then USB and Jack 3.5.


  


Finish with the two leds, which need a special preparation.

IR led with traverse copper, it must be folded at 180°. Take care of polarity !



Green led 3mm is mounted on copper side, and its pins are folded.

It comes on CMS place. This led can be replaced by a CMS version, step 1206.


 

 

Boitier

Le schéma des découpes et mesures est disponible au format PDF.

Le boîtier devra être perçé à 3mm pour la led verte, 6mm pour la led IR et 10 ou 12mm pour les deux capteurs.





Le montage final demande aussi une découpe carrée pour le connecteur USB ainsi qu'un perçage latéral de 6mm pour le connecteur Jack.
