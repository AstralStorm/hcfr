HCFR Colorimeter

Generalities
 

HCFR colorimeter probe DIY building is relatively easy, except two points: CMS chips soldering, and initial PIC programming, which need a programmator device.

 




On this photo, you can see the two TAOS sensors, green led indication power, infrared led (blue) and at the bottom, the USB port.

A zip archive with all documents is available here.

It includes Copper Gerber file, holes drill file for those who would need it ;)
