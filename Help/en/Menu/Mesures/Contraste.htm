HCFR Colorimeter

 

Measures menu

 

The measures menu launches measures and handles main document parameters. Its options match buttons in Measures, Extended measures and Saturation measures toolbars.

 

Contrast

This option measures On/Off and ANSI contrast.
 


 

Before measuring ANSI contrast, the probe must be centered on a checkerboard square to have reliable results. To display the checkerboard with the View images generator, you can use the checkerboard button inside measures view.

There is no graph displaying contrast values. The on/off contrast is visible in measures view title when grayscale is selected, and detailed values are displayed in the contrast grid.
