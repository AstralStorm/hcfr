Gamma graph

The gamma graph display luminance values computed from gray scale measures.....

It can help adjusting gamma.
 

Importance of a good adjustment of gamma:

That makes it possible to emerge the blacks between 0 and 30 IRE (to have detail in the scenes dark) and not to burn the white between 70 and 100 IRE (loss of details in luminous scenes).
