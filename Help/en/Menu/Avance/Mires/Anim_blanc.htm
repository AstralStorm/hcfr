Test patterns

 
 

Animated white level test pattern

 

This option posts a test card animated on white zone. It is available to only leave of a document whose generator is “View images”.

 

 

 

This option posts two very clear vertical bars (IRE 98 and IRE 99) which move on a white zone (IRE 100). That makes it possible to adjust the total contrast of a diffuser so that them two bars are just visible (the bar of the left, clearest, must be hardly discernible).

The white pattern size depends on size defined in View images generator options

 

 

This test card is more practical than measurement in the vicinity of the white to regulate quickly its diffuser, even if it does not allow any quantified measurement, because it is enough to post it and to exploit the adjustments by observing the result directly.

When the generator is in mode GDI, this test card is sensitive to the level of exit 0-255 or 16-235 (in mode Overlay, the standard imposes scale 16-235, it thus does not have there no differences).
