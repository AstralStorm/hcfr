Test patterns

 
 

Animated black level test pattern

 

This option posts a test card animated on white zone. It is available to only leave of a document whose generator is “View images”.

 

 

 

This option posts a test card animated on black background. It is available to only leave of a document whose generator is “View images”. This option posts two very dark vertical bars (IRE 1 and IRE 2) which move on a black bottom (IRE 0). That makes it possible to adjust the total luminosity of a diffuser so that them two bars are just visible (the bar of the left, darkest, must be hardly discernible).

Unlike Animated white test pattern, this one always use full screen size, regardless of patterns size option in View image generator.

 

 

This test card is more practical than measurement in the vicinity of the black to regulate quickly its diffuser, even if it does not allow any quantified measurement, because it is enough to post it and to exploit the adjustments by observing the result directly.

When the generator is in mode GDI, this test card is sensitive to the level of exit 0-255 or 16-235 (in mode Overlay, the standard imposes scale 16-235, it thus does not have there no differences).
