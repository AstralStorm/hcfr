HCFR Colorimeter

 

Advanced / Simultaneous measures

 

Introduction

This menu launches simultaneous measures between many colorimeters supported by this software. To do this, you need as many opened documents than different colorimeters plugged in the PC.

Commercial colorimeters are supported for one per model. You can have many HCFR probes simultaneously, but in this case, you must manually fill the COM port for each probe to make the software recognize them as different probes (real COM ports can be found in the list of peripherals connected to the PC).

The simulated sensor can be set in many units (only for test, those measures are of course no practical interest).

The generator used is the one selected in the active document when the menu is called.

 

Primary colors

This option launches primary colors simultaneous measures on all opened documents.
 


 

The primary color measures are visible in measures view and in CIE diagram.

 

 

Note: the led of Colorimeters HCFR implied in this process remains extinct during all duration of measurements, provided that they have a firmware of version 5.20 or later.
